How to play

  • Start typing to search for the album title that you think may be the answer
  • Submit your answer to see if you are correct
  • If you are incorrect you will see a slightly less pixelated version of the album cover
  • Try another answer to see if you are correct this time
  • Keep trying until you find the answer or you have used all 6 attemps
  • Don't forget to share to your friends when you find the answer!
